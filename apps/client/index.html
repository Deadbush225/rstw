Field manual

How to answer the signal

One readable objective chain, with room for faster routes and coordinated counterplay.

  1. 01
    Scramble

    Steer with WASD, jump over Storm Spinners, and dive through openings.

  2. 02
    Prepare

    Grab the Rescue Crate and park it on the Pump to delay the flood.

  3. 03
    Restore

    Hold the central Weather Relay until the Resilience Core becomes available.

  4. 04
    Recover

    Press F beside the Core, then return it to your team's Bayanihan Beacon.
